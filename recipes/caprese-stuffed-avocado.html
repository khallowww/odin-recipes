Recipe for Caprese Stuffed Avocado

How to make caprese stuffed avocado

This Caprese stuffed avocado salad is made up of avocado halves, filled with a simple Caprese salad using cherry tomatoes, mozzarella pearls, and basil, and drizzled with balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut in half
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella pearls
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps for perfect Caprese stuffed avocado

  1. Arrange avocado halves onto a plate
  2. Toss together mozzarella pearls, cherry tomatoes, and basil in a small bowl
  3. Place pit side down onto the grill. Grill for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks
  4. Spoon the Caprese filling into each avocado half and drizzle with balsamic vinegar
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Serve immediately
